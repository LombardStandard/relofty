About us

Project

The housing landscape in the community-living sector in Italy presents some delays and structural problems. After analyzing the main companies operating in the sector, we have mainly identified 3 directions of work:

  • Combining real estate development with a dedicated and special attention to the various communities
  • Strongly characterize the housing offer from a technological and sustainable point of view
  • Interpreting the Italian spirit of excellence and sociability, made of long tradition and housing diversity

So Relofty wants to be a smart, sustainable and purely Italian place.

Team. In the period 2000-2012 the team founded and managed the first structured and thematic co-living in the center of Milan, with an important local and international community focused on design, fashion, art and architecture. The project received institutional and commercial recognition from the Municipality of Milan, Triennale di Milano, mainstream media and other cultural institutions. In 2023 we intend to repeat that experience with the involvement of young generations linked to technological, ecological issues and new forms of sociality.

Vision. A new way of sharing daily spaces in which to live and work, which allows us to break the housing logic of contemporary metropolises, giving us the opportunity to grow, compare and fulfil ourselves in the most suitable places. The goal is to connect the real estate sector, and its constructive and profit logic, with the various communities of digital natives, united by similar ideals and passions, allowing both to realise their interests.
